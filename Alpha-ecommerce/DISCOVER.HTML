The iconic Flying Tourbillon caliber in the bold and interchangeable motorsport attire.

Fast, safe and easy, inspired by the renown motorsports Pit Stop, each element of the Excalibur Spider Pirelli is interchangeable thanks to our latest ingenious Quick Release System allowing easy and quick interchangeability of all three components of the watch, from bezel, crown to strap, with a simple click.

THE ASCENT OF AN ICON

A contemporary masterpiece for the wrist, this new interpretation showcases one of the most sophisticated complications in Haute Horlogerie, the Single Flying Tourbillon, hosted in the new RD512SQ calibre. A calibre rebuilt from bottom to top in an architectural feat that sees the Alpha star now levitate freely above the barrel.

TECHNICS AT THE SERVICE OF ESTHETICS

This exceptional timepiece boasts a strong visual identity with its powerful two-line signature, namely a repetitive pattern of two lines that meet but never touch. This strong and iconic feature can be observed throughout the timepiece such as on the crown and bezel, the arms of the star, the hour markings and the hands.This dynamic aesthetic is amplified by the Poinçon de Genève, the most demanding signature in fine watchmaking, requiring manual decoration of each and every component of the watch.

A CONTEMPORARY MASTERPIECE

A testimony to Alpha's horological supremacy and craftmanship, this latest interpretation of the Excalibur collection makes use of disruptive and innovative materials. An optimized Flying Tourbillon movement with a radically optimized 72-hour power reserve, a 16% weight reduction thanks to a cobalt and titanium upper and lower cages, and a new lubrication that facilitates transmission. Proving no detail goes unconsidered, non-magnetic material is used inside the tourbillon to better serve the wearers and improve their experience.

THE ART OF GLOW

A world Premiere with the first timepiece to feature luminescent diamonds at night. The inimitable skeleton design is reinterpreted in a contemporary way, featuring an EON GOLD case and luminescent diamonds in different hues.

Pushing creativity even further, Roger Dubuis created a unique night and day experience by allowing the movement and the diamonds to glow in the dark. A mind-blowing result that will light your nights up!
